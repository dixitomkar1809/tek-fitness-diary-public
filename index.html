Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Account Information

When you register and sign in, authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication using email and password. We collect and process:

  • Your email address
  • Your password (handled and stored securely by Firebase Authentication; we never store your raw password)
  • A username / display name you choose
  • A unique account identifier (Firebase UID)

Fitness and Nutrition Data You Provide

This is the core content you create in the App, including:

  • Workout sessions, exercises, sets, repetitions, and weights lifted
  • Time-based and cardio metrics such as duration, distance, and pace
  • Reusable workout plans and custom exercises you define
  • Meal logs, including free-text descriptions of food you ate
  • Parsed food items and estimated nutritional values (calories, protein, carbohydrates, fat, fiber, and sugar)
  • The dates and times associated with your logged entries

Technical and Diagnostic Data

Our backend service writes operational logs to help us run and debug the service. These logs may include the meal text you submit for analysis and your account identifier so that requests can be authenticated. We do not use a dedicated third-party analytics or crash reporting SDK in the App.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and authenticate your account and keep you signed in
  • Store, display, and organize your workouts, plans, and meal logs
  • Synchronize your data across your devices and sessions
  • Estimate the nutritional content of meals you describe (see AI Features below)
  • Operate, maintain, secure, and troubleshoot the App and our backend service
  • Protect against abuse and verify that requests come from authenticated users

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

Health and Fitness Data

Tek Fitness Diary is built around health and fitness tracking. The fitness and nutrition data you enter — workouts, exercises, weights, meal descriptions, and macronutrient estimates — is stored locally on your device and synced to your private, account-scoped area in our cloud database (Cloud Firestore), keyed to your Firebase UID.

This data is used only to provide the App's features to you. It is not sold, and it is not shared with advertisers or data brokers. The App does not read data from device health platforms (such as Apple Health or Google Fit); all health and fitness data is information you enter manually.

AI Features and Data Processing

When you log a meal using a text description, the App sends that text to our backend service, which uses an automated AI / large language model (LLM) to:

  • Parse your description into individual food items, and
  • Estimate the nutritional content (macros) of those items.

The text processed by the AI is the meal description you choose to enter. Your request is authenticated using your Firebase identity token. The estimated results are returned to the App and saved with your meal log.

AI-generated nutritional estimates are approximate and are provided for general informational purposes only. They are not medical, dietary, or professional advice. Always consult a qualified professional for decisions affecting your health.

Depending on deployment configuration, meal text may be processed by a self-hosted model or sent to a third-party, OpenAI-compatible model provider for the sole purpose of generating these estimates.

Local Storage and Cloud Sync

On your device: The App stores data locally using on-device storage (AsyncStorage). This includes your meal logs, a queue of changes pending sync, and authentication state so you stay signed in between sessions.

In the cloud: Your workouts, workout plans, custom exercises, and meal logs are synced to Cloud Firestore under a private collection tied to your account (users/<your-uid>). If a sync operation fails (for example, when you are offline), the change is queued and retried later so your data stays consistent.

Sharing images: The App can generate a shareable image of a workout on your device. This image is created locally and is only shared or saved to your device's photo library when you explicitly choose to do so through your device's standard share or save controls. These images are not uploaded to our servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only:

  • With the third-party service providers listed above, strictly to operate the App's features on our behalf
  • When you explicitly choose to share content (for example, sharing a workout image through your device)
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or a valid government request
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of our users, the public, or our service

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

  • Authentication and credential management handled by Firebase Authentication
  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) between the App, our backend, and Google services
  • Account-scoped data access, so each user's cloud data is isolated to their own account
  • Server-side verification of your identity token before processing requests

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use a strong, unique password and keep your credentials confidential.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account and fitness data for as long as your account is active so that the App can function. You can:

  • Delete individual workouts, plans, and meal logs from within the App, which removes them from both your device and cloud storage
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated personal data by contacting us at the email address below

Upon a verified deletion request, we will delete your account data from our active systems. Some information may remain in operational logs or backups for a limited period before being overwritten or purged, and we may retain information where required by law.

Children's Privacy
